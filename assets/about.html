About This App

FindFood Capabilities

Have you ever found yourself hungry in a foreign country without the ability to recognize what around you is edible? Well, with The FindFood App you will be given the chance to identify if something is edible by simply uploading a picture!

With the FindFood App you have the ability to upload images and they will be scanned by a proprietary AI, sorted, and then displayed with their foodiness value.
